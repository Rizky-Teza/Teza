Ahmad Dahlan University

Ahmad Dahlan University is a private university in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. It was founded on November 18, 1960. Ahmad Dahlan University develops fields of expertise or study programs in the fields of technology as well as in the social and humanities fields.

FAST
